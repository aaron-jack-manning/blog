Stars and Bars - A Blog About Mathematics

Hello traveller across the internet. You have stumbled across my mathematics blog, so welcome! Ultimately, I post something here because something has been on my mind about maths education, I've come across a beautiful problem worth sharing, or I want to explore a given topic and approaches to explaining and understanding it. The name stars and bars comes from a simple yet very elegant approach to the combinatorics problem of combinations with reputation, which I believe illustrates how considering a new perspective on a problem can turn it from something complex and confusing to be intuitive, immediately understandable and beautiful. If you're new here, I would recommend reading this post here to start. .
